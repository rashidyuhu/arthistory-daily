# Privacy Policy for ArtHistory Daily

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

ArtHistory Daily ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

ArtHistory Daily is a read-only application that:
- Does not require user registration or login
- Does not collect names, emails, or any personal data
- Does not track user behavior or usage patterns
- Does not use analytics or tracking services
- Does not store any user-generated content

## Data Usage

The app displays artwork information from the National Gallery of Art's Open Data program. This data is:
- Publicly available and open access
- Provided under CC0 (Creative Commons Zero) license
- Downloaded from the National Gallery of Art's official repository

## Third-Party Services

ArtHistory Daily fetches artwork data from:
- National Gallery of Art Open Data (https://github.com/NationalGalleryOfArt/opendata)
- Your artwork data endpoint (configured in the app)

No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13, as we do not collect any information from any users.
